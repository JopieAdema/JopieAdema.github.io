--- layout: home title: Joop Adema subtitle: Post-doc at the University of Innsbruck, CESifo affiliate and RF Berlin research affilate --- My research lies at the intersection of labor economics and political economy, with a focus on three themes. First, I study how migrants make decisions — what information they use, how technology shapes their choices, and what drives integration outcomes. Second, I examine how host societies respond to migration: what determines public support, and how policy can foster sustainable migration without generating backlash. Third, I am committed to reproducible research and actively contribute to the replication movement, which I see as essential to making economics credible as a policy-relevant discipline.
